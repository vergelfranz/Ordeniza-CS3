Climate Change: Information and Prevention

Climate Change: Cause and Effect

Climate change adversely influences our ecosystems in the worst ways possible. Climate change is an utmost concern as it could deliberately harm the different parts of our only home. I came up with this topic to share some pieces of information and prevention on climate change. Therefore, let us try our hardest to restore its beauty.

CAUSES

Generating electricity and heat by burning fossil fuels causes a large chunk of global emissions. The manufacturing industry is one of the largest contributors to greenhouse gas emissions worldwide. Deforestation, together with agriculture and other land use changes, is responsible for roughly a quarter of global greenhouse gas emissions. Most cars, trucks, ships, and planes run on fossil fuels. That makes transportation a major contributor to greenhouse gases, especially carbon-dioxide emissions. Food production is a major contributor to climate change. And greenhouse gas emissions also come from packaging and distributing food. So does the consumption of goods such as clothing, electronics, and plastics. A large chunk of global greenhouse gas emissions are linked to private households. Our lifestyles have a profound impact on our planet.

EFFECTS

As greenhouse gas concentrations rise, so does the global surface temperature. Destructive storms have become more intense and more frequent in many regions. (Including Philippines!) Many people now face the threat of not having enough water on a regular basis as llimate change is changing water availability, making it scarcer in more regions. Changes in the climate and increases in extreme weather events are among the reasons behind a global rise in hunger and poor nutrition. Lastly poses risks to the survival of living organisms both on land and in the ocean.

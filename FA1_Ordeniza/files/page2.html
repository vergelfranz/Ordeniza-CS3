Hotel del Luna

  • The series focuses on an ephemeral hotel that is open to the spirits of those who still have unfinished business in the earthly realm.
  • A supernatural place, the hotel is not visible in its true form during the daytime and humans can only come across the hotel under special circumstances.
  • Due to a huge sin committed more than a millennium ago, the hotel catering to the dead has been bound to Jang Manwol's soul, the main character.

Twenty-Five Twenty-One

  • In a time when dreams seem out of reach, a teen fencer pursues big ambitions and meets a hardworking young man who seeks to rebuild his life.
  • At ages 22 and 18, they say each other's names for the first time, and at ages 25 and 21, they fall in love. That's why the series called Twenty-five Twenty-one.
  • Na Hee-do's daughter, quits ballet and "runs away" to her grandma's house. During her stay, she comes across her mother's diary, through which the story is then told.
